Hello, Hexor!

2018-04-24

Hexor is an iOS app created for the users of the hexo blog.
It allows you to read, bookmark, and share the bits and pieces you have recorded on your own blog.

What is Hexo

Hexo, is a fast, concise and efficient blogging framework.

What is Hexor

Hexor is an iOS app developed for this framework.

Maybe your blog is only used to record some technical articles, or some life trivia.
However, it is undeniable that after the release, you will rarely read it again, especially on mobile phones.

So Hexor, let you move the blog to your iPhone to read it at any time!

Quick start

After searching for Hexor in the App Store and opening it up, you can follow the prompts to select the topics you use for your blog, enter your blog address, and everything goes well. Your blog has now turned into an app with a good reading experience!

Advantages and disadvantages

Then you may ask, my own browser to open my blog to visit is not enough, why do you want to toss an APP?
Well, I want to tell you that the reason for doing so can bring you the following benefits:

  1. Do not have to enter the URL every time and wait for the network to load resources
    Hexor automatically caches and updates the list of latest posts you publish, and it consumes very little resources.

  2. read articles quickly and beautifully
    The reading interface is perfectly adapted to mobile reading, and on the basis of the original page, more beautification and decontamination are performed.
